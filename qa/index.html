Services

Testing

We provide an all-encompassing testing approach beginning with the development of a comprehensive test plan that defines the strategy and timeline of our testing process. Our team meticulously crafts detailed test cases and scenarios, simulating various user interactions to thoroughly examine your application from multiple perspectives. By utilizing a range of advanced test design techniques, we ensure that every aspect of your application is examined for both functionality and usability, leaving no stone unturned in our quest for perfection.

Bug Report

Our bug reporting process extends beyond simple identification and documentation. We adopt a user story perspective while writing tickets, providing a clear, detailed account of the problem that goes beyond technical jargon. This gives the development team an in-depth understanding of the issue, how it affects the user experience, and crucially, its impact on the overall stability of the product. Through clear communication and comprehensive documentation, we ensure seamless integration between the QA and development teams, making the bug resolution process efficient and effective.

Automation

We ensure every component of your application is functioning as expected, providing quick, insightful feedback on the application's overall performance and stability. This allows for faster identification of issues and swift rectifications, contributing to the delivery of a high-quality product.

Process

1

Discovery

To begin, we dive into all available resources, from user manuals to SRS documents. This allows us to deeply understand the application and its intended functions. We are thorough, ensuring we have a comprehensive picture of your application's scope and expectations

2

Adhoc / Exploratory testing

We navigate the uncharted territories of your application through high-level ad hoc testing. This allows us to explore the app's ins and outs, uncover hidden defects, and familiarize ourselves with the user experience, exposing potential issues before theyescalate.

3

Test Plan and Test Cases

After doing an exploration of the app we will write the process of testing and how testing will be conducted. Intricate steps that are called test cases which will be taken by a user to navigate the app will be documented and used to test the app again at a detailed level

4

Automated testing

We use tools like Selenium and Testim to write automated tests that will cover all the application functionalities and ensure everything is functioning as it is supposed to.

5

Regress testing

When new code is introduced, we carry out regression testing to ensure that the existing functionality remains intact. We check for any adverse effects or new bugs that might have surfaced, ensuring that the application's health is not compromised.

6

Bug Documentation and Issue tracker

In the case of the occurrence of a bug, we will document it with the necessary steps to easily reproduce the bug and find the root issue. The issue tracker is a collection of all the bugs, it helps us to know which section of the app has the most defects and also helps us see if there is a recurring bug in the system. Finally, it helps craft the health report of the app

7

Health Report

We give a simple report to see the snapshot of the app and how the app is doing. This concise yet informative report offers a glimpse into your application's status, highlighting the areas of concern and the overall health of your application. This ensures you're always informed and prepared for the next steps.
